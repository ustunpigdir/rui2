Services

Our Services

Exports

For our foreign business partners, we supply the products they need from one or several different suppliers from Turkey and export them on a partial or container basis in a single item.We also provide export consultancy for our manufacturers located in a few countries, especially in Turkey, and manage all the processes on their behalf.

Imports

By providing import consultancy for Importer or Wholesale trade companies in Turkey, we manage all the necessary processes on their behalf.

Transit Trade

We supply the products that our foreign business partners need from a 3rd country with our reliable sources, and send the products directly from the country of origin to our business partners at more affordable costs. According to the need, the products are brought to Turkey and sent without being nationalized.
